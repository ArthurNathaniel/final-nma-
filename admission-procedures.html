Getting your child admitted at New Mission Academy


Kindergarten 2 up to Grade 3


  1. English Language
  2. Mathematics

Grade 4-JHS 1


  1. English Language
  2. Mathematics
  3. Science

When the papers are marked and the child is successful, parents can now pick up the admission form for the child. The pupil will be given a class only when the following documents are returned to the school:


  1. Completed admission form
  2. Required Medical Report from a practitioner
  3. Laboratory tests result
  4. Immediate past report card from former school
  5. Copy of the pupil’s birth certificate or passport;
  6. One passport sized photograph.

The Admission Fee will become due to upon completion of the admission procedure. This is a one-time, non-refundable payment to be made on or before the due date for the payment as stipulated on the invoice.
