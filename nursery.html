Nursery

A world of discovery through play-based learning

At New Mission Academy, we believe that the key to early education is to provide children with a safe and nurturing environment where they feel comfortable to explore and learn confidently. We encourage and support all areas of the child's development.

We recognise and meet the individual development of each child within a happy, caring and stimulating learning environment. We aim to provide creative, engaging and age-appropriate play-based activities to promote the social, emotional, physical and intellectual development of all our creche children. Imagination, creativity, curiosity and confidence are the cornerstones of a child's experience and progress at our school.

Play is meaningful

Play is one of the most fundamental ways in which young children gain essential knowledge and skills. For this reason, we strive to ensure that your child benefits from varied and well-directed early learning opportunities through their involvement in a wide range of carefully planned educational play-based activities in a new and vibrant environment, both indoors and outdoors.

A home away from home

Our small class sizes and an inspiring and highly qualified teaching faculty ensure the children are getting the best possible care at this vital stage of their learning journey. We believe that a low student-teacher ratio can have a substantial impact on your child’s education. This is why we’ve created a safe space in all our classes by limiting class sizes. All classes are facilitated by the full-time teacher and a fully qualified teaching partner in order to get to know every child, tailor activities to their individual needs, bring out the best in them and encourage greater participation in class. Our teachers help to foster a lifelong love of learning in your child and guide them to become a successful, confident, well-rounded and caring global citizen.

Parent involvement

We value the importance of establishing a solid partnership between home and school. Our learning journey with your child is a partnership, a triangle involving the child, parent and teacher. Good communication is vital in the care and education of our students. There will be a range of opportunities for involvement from reading to cooking together with your child. We encourage families to speak directly to class teachers face-to-face. Both the class teacher and teaching partner can be reached via their respective email address. All classrooms have telephones in case of urgent communication. And through our digital tools, teachers can post videos and photos of your child's Creche experience, providing feedback on how your child is progressing and communicate important information to you. Our individualised and child-centred approach goes far beyond just education. Our teachers help to foster a lifelong love of learning in your child and guide them to become a successful, confident, well-rounded and caring global citizen.
