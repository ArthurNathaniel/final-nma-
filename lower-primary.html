Early Years Learning Experience

The Early Years Programme at New Mission Academy is designed to lay the foundations for a love of lifelong learning. Through our child-centred approach, your child will be given the opportunity to drive their own learning and follow their interests, whilst developing key strengths and abilities. Your child will be engaged in play-based experiences that allow them to make connections to what they already know and encouraged to ask questions, reflect, and update their understanding of society and the world around them.

The learning experience that your child will have with us is driven by the pedagogy, nurturing independent learning skills, and encouraging every student to take responsibility for their learning.

We provide our Early Years students with a holistic learning experience that integrates social, emotional, physical and cognitive development. Our learners are engaged in stimulating inquiry-based curriculum. We provide learning experiences that are meaningful, inclusive and multi-sensory, in order to raise your child’s natural curiosity and interests through a number of engaging units of inquiry. The foundations of Mathematics and Literacy are also firmly laid here to prepare your child well for entry into Primary School.
